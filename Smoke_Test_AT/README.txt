Each time, before running Smoke Tests again, you need to 
clear the cookies and other site data in your browser. The 
information entered in the test fields is remembered and automatically 
filled in the fields, in order to reset all the information previously 
entered in the fields, you need to clear the cookie in the browser. 
If you are using Google Chorme, you need to press 
the key combination Ctrl + Shift + Del, then "Дополнительно", 
check the checkbox "Файлы cookie и другие данные сайтов" 
and click on the "Удалить данные" button
